# Visualization

[leafviz](https://github.com/jackhump/leafviz) is a shiny app to vizualize splicing results output from leafcutter.

To visualize an example without installing take a look at the [leafviz visualization](https://leafcutter.shinyapps.io/leafviz/)  for 10 brain vs. 10 heart samples from [GTEx](https://www.gtexportal.org/home/).

Written by Jack Humphrey, David A. Knowles & Yang Li.
Updated for leafcutter2 compaitiblity by Scott I. Adamson.

## Installation ##
```R
# in R:
install.packages("remotes")
remotes::install_github("scottiadamson/leafviz")
```

## Data preparation ##
The data preparation steps for taking leafcutter output and visualizing it with leafviz have been moved to commands within leafviz, which is outlined in the leafviz [README](https://github.com/scottiadamson/leafviz/tree/leafcutter2).
